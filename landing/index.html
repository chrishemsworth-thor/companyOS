Why one base, not five apps

Most companies stitch together an accounting app, a CRM, a helpdesk and a project tracker. CompanyOS is built the other way around.

⬡

One connected base, across every department

A single record of truth shared by every team — departments are a lens over the same data, never a silo with its own copy.

  • Sales and Support read the same customer — no sync, no drift
  • An invoice, its deal, and its tickets link natively in one database
  • Teams, reporting lines and departments map onto live module data
  • Management sees a cross-module overview, not four dashboards
◎

An AI layer as the operating backbone

Every business process is exposed through one normalized, machine-readable API — so AI agents are the default operators, and humans supervise from the console.

  • Autonomous agents follow up, chase and escalate on their own schedule
  • Every change flows through one event bus agents can react to
  • Collections agent watches overdue invoices and acts per customer
  • Full agent activity feed — every action visible and auditable
